About me

I'm a research scientist in Music Information Retrieval. Currently, I work at Chordify doing a bunch of different MIR stuff.

For my PhD, I looked at how hearing aids can help with tinnitus, and tested different strategies in a clinical trial. During those years, I worked at the ENT department of the UMCG in Groningen, the Netherlands, doing some psychoacoustic experiments to assess tinnitus and hearing loss.

I also make music. I've singed in different bands, such as Jini and Kinesic. But I also make my own stuff under the nerd name of Vanishing Gradient, sue me.

Among other things, I also like bouldering and comedy. I've done some amateur stand-up comedy and an improv comedy podcast, which I refuse to share here because I actually don't want you to sue me.
