Jose L. Santacruz

Research scientist
and musician.

AI | Audio | DSP | Music

Hi there! I'm a MIR researcher at Chordify.

My main research focuses on machine learning applied to audio and music. I work on a wide range of music information retrieval projects, including transcription, structure analysis, and lyrics-to-audio alignment.

I spent my PhD years at the UMCG in Groningen, the Netherlands, exploring the puzzle of tinnitus. By conducting psychoacoustic experiments and a clinical trial, I looked at how to accurately measure tinnitus, its impact on daily life, and how to manage it using personalized settings on hearing aids. Here is my thesis.

I also like to sing and play a bit of guitar. I've played in different bands, such as Jini and Kinesic. Lately, I've been also spending some time on my solo project, called Vanishing Gradient.
