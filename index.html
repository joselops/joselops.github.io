About me

I'm a researcher in the field of Music Information Retrieval. Currently, I work at Chordify doing a bunch of different MIR stuff.

I'm also writing my thesis. My PhD has been focused on hearing aids strategies to ameliorate tinnitus through a clinical trial. During the last few years, I've worked at the ENT department of the UMCG in Groningen, the Netherlands. Here, I've done some psychoacoustic experiments to assess tinnitus and hearing loss.

I sing and play a bit of guitar. I've played in different bands, such as Jini and Kinesic.

Among other things, I also like bouldering and comedy. I've done some amateur stand-up comedy and an improv comedy podcast.
